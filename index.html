ScentCast Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

ScentCast ("the App") is developed and operated by HRCWA Holdings LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We built ScentCast as a free app with no ads and no monetization. We collect minimal data and will never sell your information to third parties.

Information We Collect

Location Data
The App requests access to your device location solely to retrieve local weather conditions for fragrance recommendations. We do not store, log, or share your location data. Location is used in real time and discarded immediately after your weather data is fetched.

Device Data
We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any hardware information.

Usage Data
We do not track how you use the App, which features you access, or how long you use it.

Fragrance Data
Any fragrance preferences or collection data you enter stays on your device. We do not transmit or store this information on our servers. Fragrance preference data may be sent to OpenAI solely to generate your recommendation and is not stored or used for any other purpose.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for one purpose only: to generate personalized fragrance recommendations based on your current weather conditions and stated preferences. No data is used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any secondary purpose.

Data Storage and Security

ScentCast does not operate a user database. No personal information is transmitted to or stored on our servers. All app data remains locally on your device unless temporarily transmitted to our third party service providers as described in this policy.

Third Party Services

OpenWeatherMap
We use OpenWeatherMap to retrieve current and forecast weather conditions based on your location. Your device coordinates are sent to OpenWeatherMap solely to fetch weather data relevant to your fragrance recommendation. No personal identifiers are transmitted. You can review OpenWeatherMap's privacy policy at: https://openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy

OpenAI
We use OpenAI's API to power fragrance recommendations within the App. When you request a recommendation, relevant inputs such as weather conditions and fragrance preferences you have entered are sent to OpenAI to generate a personalized suggestion. We do not transmit your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information to OpenAI. You can review OpenAI's privacy policy at: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Apple App Store
The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data in accordance with their own privacy practices. You can review Apple's privacy policy at: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Children's Privacy

ScentCast is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to know what data we collect
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of data selling (we do not sell data)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

European Union users have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, and erase personal data, and the right to data portability.

California residents have rights under CCPA including the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the Last Updated date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
