ScentCast Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

ScentCast ("the App") is developed and operated by HRCWA Holdings LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Create or update your user profile
  • Subscribe to our daily email recommendations
  • Use the App's features and services

This information may include:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Location information (city or ZIP code for weather-based recommendations)
  • Age range (optional, for personalized suggestions)
  • Fragrance preferences and collection data

Automatically Collected Information

When you access the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (operating system, device type)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Error logs and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized fragrance recommendations based on weather and preferences
  • Send daily email notifications with fragrance suggestions (if subscribed)
  • Maintain and improve the App's functionality
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or support
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database platform. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third Party Services

ScentCast integrates with the following third-party services:

Supabase

We use Supabase for user authentication and data storage. Supabase's privacy policy can be found at: https://supabase.com/privacy

Affiliate Disclosure & Commercial Relationships

Overview

ScentCast may earn commissions when you purchase fragrances through certain retail links in our app. We participate in multiple affiliate programs to provide you with convenient shopping options at no additional cost.

Affiliate Programs We Participate In

1. Amazon Associates Program

ScentCast is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for us to earn fees by linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

When you tap "Shop on Amazon" links in our app, we may earn a small commission (typically 3-10% of the purchase price) on qualifying purchases. This comes at no extra cost to you — you pay the same price whether you use our link or not.

2. CJ Affiliate Network

We also participate in the CJ Affiliate Network (Commission Junction), through which we partner with the following fragrance retailers:

  • FragranceShop.com - We earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through our FragranceShop links
  • Perfumania - We earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through our Perfumania links

These partnerships allow us to offer you multiple shopping options and earn a commission when you make purchases through our referral links.

How Affiliate Links Work

What this means for you:

  • ✅ Same prices - You pay the same price whether you use our links or go directly to the retailer
  • ✅ No extra cost - Commissions are paid by the retailer, not added to your purchase
  • ✅ Unbiased recommendations - Our fragrance recommendations are based on weather, your preferences, and quality — never on commission rates
  • ✅ Optional - You are never required to purchase through our affiliate links
  • ✅ Multiple options - We provide links to multiple retailers so you can choose

Retailers available in the app:

  • Amazon.com (via Amazon Associates)
  • FragranceShop.com (via CJ Affiliate)
  • Perfumania (via CJ Affiliate)

Your Privacy with Affiliate Links

What we DO collect:

  • ✅ That a click occurred on an affiliate link
  • ✅ Which fragrance recommendation led to the click
  • ✅ General analytics about link performance

What we DON'T collect:

  • ❌ Personal information about your purchases
  • ❌ Credit card or payment information
  • ❌ Specific items you purchase
  • ❌ Purchase amounts or order details

Third-party tracking:

  • When you click a link, you'll be redirected through the retailer's affiliate tracking system (e.g., Amazon Associates or CJ Affiliate)
  • This allows the retailer to track that the sale came from our app
  • After the redirect, you're on the retailer's website and their privacy policy applies
  • Cookie duration: Typically 24 hours for browsing, up to 90 days if you add items to cart (varies by retailer)

Transparency Commitment

We believe strongly in transparency:

  1. Clear disclosure - All retail links in the app are affiliate links
  2. No hidden fees - You always see the same prices as going directly to the retailer
  3. Quality first - We recommend fragrances based on merit, not commission potential
  4. Your choice - You can always search for fragrances yourself without using our links

How Commissions Support ScentCast

The commissions we earn help us:

  • Keep the app free to use
  • Cover server costs (Supabase, OpenAI API)
  • Continue developing new features
  • Maintain the fragrance database
  • Provide daily personalized recommendations

Without affiliate income, we would need to charge for the app or use intrusive advertising.
